Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play multiplayer tactical first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. The game emphasizes precise gunplay, strategic team compositions, and character abilities. Players control "agents" with unique skills, collaborating in teams to either plant or defuse a bomb on diverse maps.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox game that allows players to build and explore blocky 3D worlds. Whether you're into survival mode, where you fight against monsters and gather resources, or creative mode, where you have unlimited resources to create anything, Minecraft is a game of limitless possibilities. With a vibrant community and a huge variety of mods and custom servers, Minecraft continues to captivate players of all ages.
